Health Insights

Understand Your Pulse Better

Monitor your heart health effectively. Learn about your pulse rate, what it means, and how to maintain a balanced heartbeat through lifestyle changes and awareness.

Pulse rate measures the number of heartbeats per minute — it reflects how well your heart is functioning. A steady, consistent pulse shows good heart rhythm.

A normal resting pulse for adults is between 60–100 bpm. Athletes often have a lower rate, between 40–60 bpm, due to stronger heart muscles.

If your pulse is irregular, too fast, or too slow, it may be a sign of heart conditions or dehydration. Always consult your doctor if irregularities persist.

Stay hydrated, exercise regularly, avoid smoking, and manage stress to maintain a healthy heart rhythm. A balanced lifestyle keeps your pulse in check.
