Course Overview

A practical, hands-on training program designed to give you complete mastery of Shopify. Over 4 days, you’ll set up a store, customize themes, add products, configure payments, and launch marketing campaigns.

Why Choose This Course?

Expert Trainers

Learn directly from professionals with years of Shopify development experience.

Industry-Relevant Skills

Certification

Shopify Admin & Settings

Who Is This Course For?

Entrepreneurs, marketers, business managers, or anyone wanting to run their own online store. No technical background required — just the passion to grow your e-commerce brand.

Day-by-Day Curriculum

Day 1

Store setup, Admin overview, domain configuration, and planning.

Day 2

Theme structure, sections, UX customization, and Liquid basics.

Day 3

Products & collections, payments, shipping, apps & automation.

Day 4

SEO foundations, campaigns, analytics, and final project review.
